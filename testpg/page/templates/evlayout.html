Workshop Overview

OBJECTIVE:

We all know how EVs are progressing. They are currently the dominant technologies in the new generation of automobiles. This workshop examines the newest major sub-systems of hybrid and electrified vehicles, including the engine, as well as the integration of these sub-systems into the vehicle as a whole.
